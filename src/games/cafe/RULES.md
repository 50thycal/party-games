# Cafe - Complete Game Rules

## Overview

**Cafe** is a competitive cafe management game where players run their own coffee shops. Each round, you'll buy supplies, draft customers, fulfill orders, and pay rent. Manage your resources wisely, delight customers for bonuses, and avoid going bankrupt. The player with the most money after 5 rounds (or the last one standing) wins!

---

## Quick Start Summary

| Element | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Players** | 2-4 players |
| **Goal** | Have the most money after 5 rounds |
| **Win Condition** | Highest money total, or last player standing |
| **Starting Money** | $40 per player |
| **Rent** | $10 per round |
| **Rounds** | 5 total rounds |

---

## Game Setup

When the game begins:

1. **Each player receives:**
   - $40 starting money
   - 3 upgrade cards in hand
   - Empty supply storage (no starting supplies)

2. **Shared reputation track** starts at 0 (neutral)

3. **Customer deck** is shuffled (52 total customer cards across 5 archetypes)

4. **Customers per round:** 3 per player (2 players = 6, 3 players = 9, 4 players = 12)

---

## Round Structure

Each round follows these phases in order:

### 1. Planning Phase
- Review your current resources and prepare for the round
- All players click "Ready" to advance

### 2. Investment Phase
During this phase, you can spend money on:

**Buy Supplies ($1 each):**
- Coffee Beans
- Tea
- Milk
- Syrup

**Activate Upgrade Cards:**
- Play upgrade cards from your hand (max 3 active)
- Coffee Machine upgrades stack for free resources each round

**Claim Coffee Machine Bonus:**
- If you have coffee machine upgrades installed, claim free supplies

All players click "Ready" when finished investing.

### 3. Customer Draft Phase
Customers are dealt one at a time in a **pass-or-take** draft:

1. **Drawer** reveals a customer card (shows archetype on front)
2. **Drawer decides:** Take the customer or pass
3. **If passed:** Next player decides (take or pass)
4. **Full circle:** If everyone passes, customer returns to drawer who must take it
5. **Repeat** until all customers for the round are distributed

**Important:** You only see the customer's archetype (front) when deciding. The specific order requirements (back) are hidden until resolution!

### 4. Customer Resolution Phase
Now the orders are revealed! For each customer you took:

1. **Review the order** - See what supplies are required
2. **Decide to fulfill or skip** - Toggle which customers to serve
3. **All players confirm** their choices simultaneously

**Fulfilling an Order:**
- Pay the required supplies
- Receive the money reward
- Customer outcome determined (delighted, satisfied, or stormed out)

**Skipping an Order:**
- Customer storms out (reputation penalty)
- Storm out effects may apply (archetype-specific penalties)

### 5. Shop Closed Phase
- Review the results of customer service
- See who was delighted, satisfied, or stormed out
- All players click "Ready" to proceed

### 6. Cleanup Phase
**Pay Rent:** Every player must pay $10 rent

- **Can afford:** Money is deducted automatically
- **Can't afford:** Go bankrupt and are eliminated!
- **Bailout option:** Other players can pay your rent for you

After rent, a new round begins (first drawer rotates).

## Customer Archetypes (5 Types)

### Average Joe (12 cards)
**Easy to please, reliable business**
- Simple orders (black coffee, tea)
- Delight: Serve multiple Average Joes in a round
- Storm out: Usually no penalty

### Coffee Snob (12 cards)
**Demands quality, high risk/reward**
- Complex coffee orders requiring multiple supplies
- Delight: Have surplus coffee beans after serving
- Storm out: May lose money ($2-$5)

### Influencer (8 cards)
**Here for the aesthetic, affects reputation**
- Varied orders with presentation focus
- Delight: Have all 4 supply types in stock
- Storm out: Extra reputation loss (-2 instead of -1)

### Health Person (10 cards)
**Wellness focused, tea preference**
- Tea-based orders
- Delight: Have surplus tea after serving
- Storm out: May lose tea supplies

### Bulk Orderer (10 cards)
**Big orders, big payouts**
- Large orders requiring many supplies
- Delight: Have 3+ total supplies after serving
- Storm out: Significant money loss ($5)

---

## Customer Outcomes

When you fulfill a customer order, one of three outcomes occurs:

### Delighted
- **Reputation +1** (shared track moves up)
- **Bonus effect** based on the specific card:
  - Bonus money (tips)
  - Bonus reputation (+1 extra)
  - Bonus supplies
  - Draw upgrade cards
  - Supply refunds

### Satisfied
- Order completed normally
- No reputation change
- Just receive the money reward

### Stormed Out
- **Reputation -1** (or more for Influencers)
- **Storm out penalty** based on card:
  - Lose money
  - Lose supplies
  - Extra reputation loss

---

## Reputation Track

The reputation track is **shared by all players** and ranges from -5 to +5:

| Reputation | Customer Modifier |
|------------|-------------------|
| -5 | -4 customers per round |
| -4 | -3 customers |
| -3 | -2 customers |
| -2 | -1 customers |
| -1 to +1 | No change |
| +2 | +1 customers |
| +3 | +2 customers |
| +4 | +3 customers |
| +5 | +4 customers per round |

**Higher reputation = more customers = more opportunity (or more to manage!)**

## Upgrade Cards

### Card Limits
- **Hand size:** Max 3 cards
- **Active upgrades:** Max 3 (non-coffee machine)
- **Coffee machine:** Unlimited stacking
- **Draw:** 1 card per round (plus bonus draws from delighted customers)

### Upgrade Categories

**Efficiency (5 cards)** - Reduce costs, increase output
- Bulk Supplier Contract, Efficient Brewing, Staff Training, etc.

**Capacity (5 cards)** - Increase limits, storage
- Extra Storage, Expanded Seating, Second Register, etc.

**Reputation (4 cards)** - Affect reputation gains/losses
- Social Media Presence, Customer Apology Card, etc.

**Specialty (6 cards)** - Unique effects
- Barista Championship, Tea Ceremony Master, VIP Treatment, etc.

**Coffee Machine (6 cards)** - Stack for free resources
- Each installed card grants +1 free resource during investment
- Cards: Premium Grinder, Steam Wand, Temperature Control, Dual Boiler, Auto-Dosing, Water Filtration

## Winning & Losing

### Victory Conditions
The game ends after 5 rounds OR when only 1 player remains.

**Winner Determination:**
1. **Last player standing** - If all others go bankrupt
2. **Highest score after 5 rounds** - Score = Money + (Prestige Ã— 2)

### Elimination
- If you can't pay $10 rent at the end of a round, you're **eliminated**
- Other players may **bail you out** by paying your rent
- Once eliminated, you can only spectate

### Tie Breaker
- If scores are equal, the player with more money wins
- If still tied, multiple winners are possible

## Strategy Tips

### Early Game
1. **Buy supplies conservatively** - You don't know what orders you'll get
2. **Draft safe customers** - Average Joes are reliable income
3. **Save money for rent** - Always keep $10+ buffer

### Mid Game
1. **Watch the reputation track** - High reputation means more customers
2. **Invest in coffee machine** - Free resources compound over time
3. **Know your archetypes** - Learn which customers match your supplies

### Late Game
1. **Calculate carefully** - Count your supplies vs. potential orders
2. **Skip strategically** - Sometimes it's better to let a customer storm out
3. **Consider bailouts** - Keeping opponents in the game can benefit you (more customers to draft)

### General Tips
- **Diverse supplies** help with Influencer delight conditions
- **Surplus supplies** trigger many delight bonuses
- **Reading opponents** - Watch what supplies others buy to predict their draft behavior
- **The draft is key** - Good drafting wins games more than anything else
